# Vision

HadionERP exists to become the world's most connected construction-focused ERP platform.

It is not designed to imitate existing enterprise software.

It is designed to remove fragmentation between departments.

---

# Enterprise Principles

Business Objects before Screens

Consistency before Creativity

Configuration before Customization

Enterprise Scale before Shortcuts

Context before Navigation

Business Decisions before Data Entry

Connected Information before Duplicate Information

Lifecycle before Transactions

Financial Truth before Operational Convenience

---

# Enterprise Experience

Users should never ask:

"Which module contains this?"

Instead they should ask:

"Which business object am I looking for?"

Everything begins from the object.

---

# Product Promise

Every screen answers:

What?

Why?

Status?

Financial Impact?

Operational Impact?

Connections?

Next Step?

If not,

the screen is incomplete.
