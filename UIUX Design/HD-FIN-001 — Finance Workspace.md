# Purpose

The Finance Workspace is the financial command center of HadionERP.

Its responsibility is not only bookkeeping.

Its responsibility is to present the complete financial position of the enterprise in real time.

Every financial transaction created anywhere inside HadionERP eventually becomes visible here.

Finance is the financial truth of the organization.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Finance Philosophy

Traditional ERP

Accounting records transactions.

↓

Produces reports.

↓

Month-end analysis.

Rejected.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

HadionERP

Operations occur.

↓

Financial impact calculated immediately.

↓

Management sees live position.

↓

Accounting validates.

↓

Financial statements always ready.

Finance becomes proactive rather than reactive.
